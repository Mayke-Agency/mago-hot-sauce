About Mago

Worldwide flavor, local soul.

Founded in Laguna Beach, Mago was created with a simple mission: to make hot sauces that focus on real flavor, clean ingredients, and quality over shortcuts. Every sauce is made with natural ingredients and without preservatives, fillers, or common allergens — crafted to be something everyone can enjoy.
